My Hobbies

Sewing

One hobby I have is sewing. I learned how to sew when I was 10 when my aunt came over to fix her pants. She showed me how to sew by hand by cutting out a piece of my doll's dress and letting me sew it back together. A few months after that my mom bought me a toy sewing machine, but we couldn't get it to work right so I went to my grandma's house and she gave me one of her sewing machines to have. This is how I got really interested in sewing.

After I got pretty good at sewing I started to make clothes for my doll. The first thing I made for her was a really simple skirt. It wasn't really that good, but I was happy. Then, I made a cloth doll. It took me a few days to make her, but I was happy once I was done. It was far from perfect, but I was really proud and so was my mom. Recently, I made a suspender skirt for my doll and one for myself. These are some things I’ve made. If you would like to start sewing, here's a link to buy your own sewing machine.

Drawing

Another hobby I have is drawing. I always liked to draw, but I didn't really do it as much or was as good as I am now. I started getting very intersted in being a good drawer in 8th grade art class. I always took very long on my drawings because I wanted to get 100, but all that time I spent got me very interested in it. I started drawing a lot more at home in my free time and after a while my art started to look really good and I loved it. This is how I got intrested in drawing.

After my drawings started getting really good I started drawing more and more. I drew myself for art class and it looked nice. I also drew the "Stary Night" and it took me a very long time, but it came out amazing. Now since I got a lot better I started trying to draw cartoons of real people instead of just regular cartoons. These were two of my favorite hobbies and how I got interested in them. If you'd like to start drawing, here's a link to buy some different art supplies.
